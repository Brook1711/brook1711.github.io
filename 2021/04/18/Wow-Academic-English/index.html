Compare words 比较两个词汇

  • it is a/an apple
  • receive/accept education
  • I come/came from/for/to school

0 ~ many characters 多字母替换

Any length of characters.

Examples:

  • what are you $ing
  • I am a w$n
  • I love $melon

1 word 下划线代替单个词汇

Any single word.

Examples:

  • she is _
  • are you _ well
  • he a man

0 ~ many words 替换单个或多个词

Any number of words.

Examples:

  • do you *
  • * so hot
  • is * me

synonyms 同义词

Any words with the same meaning.

Examples:

  • I am ~happy
  • I finished my ~homework
  • ~take a photo

Optional 是否提出该词

Return results with/without the word.

Examples:

  • discuss ?about the issue
  • what ?is the problem
  • I ?am come from n.
